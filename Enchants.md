<h3>Excavation</h3>

> Mines a 3x3x1 area instead of 1x1x1, but you mine a bit slower. Sneak while mining to stop this effect.

**Max Level:** `1`

**Compatible with:**

```
Axes, Pickaxes, Shovels, Hoes
```
